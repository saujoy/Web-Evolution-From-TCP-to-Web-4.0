Web 3.0: The Semantic (Decentralized) Web

Web 3.0 is the next evolution of the web. It focuses on decentralization (blockchain), artificial intelligence, and the "Semantic Web," where computers can understand data just as well as humans do. It aims to give users more control over their own data.
